# Helios Research Standards

**Objective:** Ensure all information published by Helios is unbiased, validated, and technically accurate.

## 1. The Verification Protocol
Every piece of intelligence must pass through three stages:
1. **Data Acquisition:** Sourcing from official manufacturer datasheets, regulatory bodies (e.g., Ofgem), and technical whitepapers.
2. **Technical Validation:** Verifying efficiency ratings, compatibility, and performance claims against independent test results where available.
3. **Bias Check:** Auditing content to ensure no manufacturer or installer preference is being subtly promoted.

## 2. Primary Data Sources
- Manufacturer Spec Sheets (Inverters, Batteries, PV Modules).
- UK Government Energy Statistics (DESNZ).
- Independent energy price comparison data.
- Verified consumer reviews (unbiased).

## 3. Output Format
All research findings must be documented in a structured format:
- **Subject:** (e.g., Tesla Powerwall 3)
- **Claimed Spec:** (e.g., 5.0kWh)
- **Verified Spec:** (e.g., 5.0kWh)
- **Evidence Source:** (Link/Doc Path)
- **Confidence Score:** (1-10)
