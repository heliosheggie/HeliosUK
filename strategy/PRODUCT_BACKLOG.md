# Helios Product Backlog: The Vision

**Mission:** Empower homeowners with transparent, validated, AI-curated energy intelligence.

## 1. Solar Intelligence Engine (Core)
*   **Purpose:** Trusted, personalised solar potential.
*   **Features:** Weather/Solar Forecast (Open-Meteo, NASA), Historical Irradiance, Seasonal Yield, Shading estimation.
*   **Output:** kWh/m²/day, Annual generation, Best months.

## 2. Roof & Panel Designer (Interactive)
*   **Purpose:** Visualizing solar potential on actual roofs.
*   **Features:** Satellite image upload, Polygon drawing (Leaflet.js/Mapbox), Auto-detect pitch/orientation, Panel drag-and-drop.
*   **Output:** Max panel count, Total wattage, Estimated ROI.

## 3. Solar & Grant Calculator (Financial)
*   **Purpose:** Unbiased financial planning.
*   **Features:** System cost estimates, Annual savings, Payback period, Carbon savings, Grant eligibility (ECO4, Home Energy Scotland, etc.).

## 4. Tariff & Supplier Optimiser (Utility)
*   **Purpose:** Maximizing savings via smart tariffs.
*   **Features:** Octopus Agile/Go/Cosy comparison, Battery charging windows, EV integration.
*   **Revenue:** Integrated Octopus referral link implementation.

## 5. Full Ecosystem Simulator (Advanced)
*   **Purpose:** Holistic view of Solar + Battery + EV + Tariff.
*   **Features:** Annual grid import/export, Self-consumption %, Savings vs. No-Solar.

## 6. Support & Growth Layer (Monetisation & Engagement)
*   **Monetisation:** Stripe donations, "Buy Me a Coffee", Installer partnerships.
*   **Engagement:** Live Home Energy Dashboard, Live Solar Forecast Widget, Battery Sizing Wizard, EV Optimiser, SEG Export Comparison, Installer Quote Checker, AI "Ask Helios" Widget.
