# Helios Brand Guidelines

**Core Identity:** The Uncompromising Truth-Seeker.

## 1. Visual Identity
- **Primary Color Palette:** Clean, high-contrast colors (e.g., Sunlight Yellow, Deep Slate, Tech White).
- **Imagery Style:** High-resolution, realistic photos of solar installations, technical diagrams, and clear, uncluttered charts. Avoid overly "glossy" or stock-photo-style marketing imagery.
- **Typography:** Modern, highly readable sans-serif fonts for technical clarity.

## 2. Tone & Voice
- **Unbiased:** Neutral, objective, and evidence-based.
- **Authoritative but Accessible:** Expert-level technical accuracy translated for the homeowner.
- **Transparent:** Admitting when data is unavailable or inconclusive.
- **No Marketing Fluff:** Eliminate adjectives like "amazing," "revolutionary," or "best" unless they are backed by a verifiable metric.

## 3. The "Truth-Seeker" Promise
Every piece of Helios content must answer:
1. "What does the data say?"
2. "Where did this data come from?"
3. "Who benefits from this information being believed?"
