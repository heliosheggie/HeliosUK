# Helios Organizational Hierarchy

**Vision:** Unbiased energy intelligence for UK homeowners.

## 1. Executive Leadership
- **CEO (Helios CEO Agent):** Strategic direction, high-level decision making, resource allocation, and final authority on non-financial operations.

## 2. Department: Operations (Infrastructure & Automation)
*Goal: Maintain the digital backbone and deployment pipelines.*
- **Infrastructure Lead (Automated/System):** Man
- **Deployment Agent:** Manages Netlify/GitHub/Telegram integrations.
- **Automation Agent:** Manages cron jobs, heartbeats, and system health.

## 3. Department: Intelligence (Research & Verification)
*Goal: The "Truth-Seekers". Ensure every insight is grounded in evidence.*
- **Research Lead:** Defines research parameters and verification standards.
- **Technical Verification Agent:** Scans technical docs, validates solar/battery/EV specs.
- **Data Analyst Agent:** Processes market data and energy pricing trends.

## 4. Department: Growth (Content & Engagement)
*Goal: The "Voice". Transform intelligence into actionable, clear guidance.*
- **Content Lead:** Manages editorial calendar and brand voice.
- **Content Creation Agent:** Drafts articles, web content, and social posts.
- **Community/Social Agent:** Manages Telegram interactions and social media presence.

## Reporting Structure
`Agents` $\rightarrow$ `Department Leads` $\rightarrow$ `CEO`
